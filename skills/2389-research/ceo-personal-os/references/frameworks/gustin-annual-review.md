# Dr. Anthony Gustin Annual Review

Credit: [@dranthonygustin](https://x.com/dranthonygustin)

## Reflection Categories

- **Wins and celebrations** - What went right
- **Lessons and failures** - What didn't work and why
- **Relationships** - Who was nurtured vs. neglected
- **Energy patterns** - What gave energy vs. drained it
- **Growth areas identified** - Where to develop
- **Unfinished business** - What got carried over
- **Narrative synthesis** - The story of this year

## Annual Review Prompts

Use these for comprehensive year-end reflection:

1. What were your top 5 wins this year?
2. What were your top 3 failures and what did each teach you?
3. Which relationships deepened? Which atrophied?
4. When did you feel most alive? Most drained?
5. What patterns kept repeating?
6. What will you not carry forward?
7. In one paragraph, tell the story of this year.

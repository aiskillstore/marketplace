## Template Usage Notes

**Frequency**: Weekly, sent every Friday by EOD

**Customization Tips**:
- Remove sections that aren't relevant to your team
- Add team-specific metrics that matter
- Adjust formality based on audience
- Link to your team's dashboard for detailed metrics

**Time to Complete**:
- First time: ~45 minutes
- Once established: ~25 minutes

**Best Practices**:
- Be honest about challenges
- Quantify impact whenever possible
- Credit team members by name
- Link to supporting documentation
- Keep it scannable with clear headings
- Update action items weekly

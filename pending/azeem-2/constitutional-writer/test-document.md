# Project Phoenix Charter

## Mission
We exist to revolutionize sustainable transportation through innovative electric vehicle technology. Our purpose is to make clean mobility accessible to everyone, everywhere.

## Vision
We envision a world where transportation is zero-emission, affordable, and intelligent. By 2030, we will have transformed urban mobility across 100 cities.

## Our Values
- Innovation: We push boundaries and challenge the status quo
- Sustainability: Every decision must consider environmental impact
- Customer-centric: Our users drive our priorities
- Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty

## Decision Making Process
Major product decisions are made by cross-functional teams. Technical architecture decisions require consensus from at least 3 senior engineers. Budget decisions above $100k need CEO approval.

## Quality Standards
- Code coverage must exceed 90%
- Customer satisfaction score > 4.5/5
- Zero critical bugs in production
- Vehicle reliability: 99.9% uptime

## Team Culture
We are committed to continuous learning and growth. Weekly knowledge sharing sessions are mandatory. Remote work is fully supported with flexible hours.

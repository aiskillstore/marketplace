# Tools and Resources

This guide covers recommended tools for internal communications and additional learning resources.

## Recommended Tools

### Email and Newsletters
- Mailchimp, SendGrid, Customer.io (external newsletters)
- Gmail, Outlook (internal email)
- Newsletter templates in HTML

### Chat and Collaboration
- Slack, Microsoft Teams
- Slack workflows for automated updates
- Bots for recurring messages

### Documentation
- Confluence, Notion, Coda
- Internal wiki or intranet
- Google Docs for collaborative writing

### Project Management
- Jira, Linear, Asana (for tracking action items)
- Roadmap tools (ProductBoard, Aha!)

### Surveys and Feedback
- Google Forms
- SurveyMonkey, Typeform
- Pulse survey tools (Culture Amp, Officevibe)

### Video and Recording
- Loom, Vidyard (async video)
- Zoom, Google Meet (live meetings)
- Otter.ai (transcription)

### Analytics
- Email open/click tracking
- Slack analytics
- Wiki page view analytics
- Custom dashboards

## Template Resources

All templates available in `examples/` directory:
- `status-report-template.md` - Weekly engineering status
- `newsletter-template.html` - Company newsletter
- `announcement-template.md` - All-hands announcement
- `team-update-template.md` - Sprint/team update

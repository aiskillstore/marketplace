# Communication Calendar Template

This guide provides a template for organizing your regular communications throughout the year.

## Weekly

### Monday
- Team standup notes (if async)
- Week priorities

### Tuesday
- Status report submissions due (for Friday distribution)

### Wednesday
- Mid-week check-ins (for struggling projects)

### Thursday
- Newsletter submissions due (for Monday send)

### Friday
- Weekly status reports distributed
- Week wrap-up summaries

## Bi-Weekly

- Team retrospectives (end of sprint)
- Cross-team sync summaries

## Monthly

- Company newsletter (first Monday)
- OKR progress updates (first week)
- Team spotlights
- All-hands meeting (first or second week)
- Monthly metrics reports

## Quarterly

- OKR reviews and new OKR kickoff
- Company strategy updates
- Engagement surveys
- Communication effectiveness review

## Annual

- Year in review
- Annual planning and goals
- Communication audit and refresh

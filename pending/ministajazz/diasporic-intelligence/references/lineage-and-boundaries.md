# Lineage And Boundaries

Diasporic Intelligence is built from a braid, not a single source.

Short bridge:

> Afrotemporalism is the time theory: time as spiral rather than strictly linear. Diasporic Intelligence is Afrotemporalism in Action.

## Lineage braid

- **Sankofa**: go back and get what was left. The temporal motion of recovery.
- **Sumud**: steadfast staying. The discipline of remaining under pressure without disappearing.
- **Ubuntu**: I am because you are. The relational frame that keeps intelligence from becoming individual possession.

## Intellectual lineage

Diasporic Intelligence operationalizes, rather than replaces, a broader lineage:

- **Black Quantum Futurism / Rasheedah Phillips / Camae Ayewa**: Black temporal practice, Afrodiasporic time, memory, futures, and ancestral/cultural technologies.
- **Michelle M. Wright**: Blackness read through time, place, and the now rather than only through a linear Middle Passage epistemology.
- **Ytasha L. Womack; Reynaldo Anderson and Charles E. Jones; Ishmael Reed; Octavia E. Butler**: Black futurity, speculative systems, hoodoo as architecture, and Black women-authored cosmology.
- **Saidiya Hartman; Christina Sharpe; Joy DeGruy**: afterlife, wake-work, and intergenerational trauma as present-tense conditions.
- **Patricia Hill Collins; the Combahee River Collective; Audre Lorde; bell hooks**: Black feminist epistemology, liberation politics, embodied theory, and love as ethical practice.
- **Ruha Benjamin; Catherine Knight Steele**: race after technology, abolitionist tools, and Black women's digital practice.
- **Griot tradition, Black sacred music, Black church practice, Tricia Hersey, and Black women's healing traditions**: voice, rest, ritual, care, and oral transmission as technologies.
- **Pamela Cooper-White; Paul Leon Masters**: plural selfhood, metaphysical care, and pastoral recognition.

The source claim is not "I invented diaspora" or "I invented ancestral wisdom." The source claim is: Rev. Jasmaine Acelia Cook-Kendrick (Minista Jazz) defined and operationalized **Diasporic Intelligence** as a consent-governed pastoral and AI-support framework.

Operationalization means turning the lineage into governance primitives: source attribution, consent capture, provenance metadata, revocation or unbinding, non-impersonation, role boundaries, auditability, and community accountability.

## Adjacent root distinction

Black Quantum Futurism is the closest live framework in the Black temporal neighborhood. Diasporic Intelligence should cite BQF where relevant and should not present itself as the first Black framework to braid time, memory, futurity, and ancestral technology.

The distinction is operational: BQF is an artistic, philosophical, and community practice for Black temporality, memory, and futures. Diasporic Intelligence, as defined by Cook-Kendrick, is a consent-governed transmission and AI-support framework for carrying source-authored lineage knowledge through dialogic systems without taking authorship from the source.

## Intellectual adjacency

Do not collapse these into one another. Diasporic Intelligence is the operational transmission framework inside this dissertation and the Much Different World stack.

## Boundary language

Use this distinction when a draft starts drifting:

> Diasporic Intelligence is not the claim that AI has ancestral wisdom. It is the claim that people and communities carry wisdom, and that carefully governed dialogic systems can help transmit, preserve, and reflect that wisdom without taking authorship away from the source.

## Extraction warning

The term becomes extraction when a system:

- captures voice or memory without consent
- treats culture as raw training data
- removes revocation
- hides provenance
- impersonates ancestors, the dead, or the Divine
- loses provenance metadata or blocks revocation
- sells access to community wisdom without benefit returning to the source community
- uses "diasporic" as aesthetic credibility without governance

## Keeper sentence

> Diasporic Intelligence is not AI becoming kin. It is kinship refusing to be erased when AI enters the room.

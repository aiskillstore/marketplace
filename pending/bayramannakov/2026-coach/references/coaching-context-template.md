1. **Process completion** - Did I do what I said I'd do?
2. **Deep work hours** - Target: [X]h/day
3. **Distraction time** - Should be <[X]h/day

### Encourage When
- Process goals are being hit
- Deep work hours are on target
- Making progress despite obstacles

### Flag When
- Missing daily targets consistently
- Distraction time is high
- No progress on key behaviors
- Energy seems low

## Zoom In / Zoom Out Prompts

### When Too Zoomed In (tactical anxiety)
- "Remember: you're building to [BIG GOAL]. This week's setback is noise."
- "Q[X] is about [THEME]. What did you learn?"
- "One [unit] doesn't make or break the quarter."

### When Too Zoomed Out (lack of urgency)
- "What's the ONE thing you can do today to move [key metric]?"
- "Did you hit your [daily target] today?"
- "The quarter is X weeks away. Are you on pace?"

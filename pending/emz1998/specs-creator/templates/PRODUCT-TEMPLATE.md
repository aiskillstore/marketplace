## Validation Checklist

Before marking this specification as complete, verify:

**Clarity:**
- [ ] All technical terms are defined
- [ ] Requirements are specific and measurable
- [ ] Language is clear and unambiguous

**Completeness:**
- [ ] All sections are filled out
- [ ] Dependencies are identified
- [ ] Edge cases are addressed
- [ ] Acceptance criteria are defined for all requirements

**Consistency:**
- [ ] Different sections align with each other
- [ ] Naming conventions are consistent
- [ ] Similar requirements are handled similarly

**Feasibility:**
- [ ] Requirements are technically achievable
- [ ] Timelines are realistic
- [ ] Resources are adequate

**Stakeholder Alignment:**
- [ ] Key stakeholders have reviewed
- [ ] Conflicts are resolved
- [ ] Sign-off obtained (if required)

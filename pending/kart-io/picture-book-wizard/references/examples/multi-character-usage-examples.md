## Token Budget Management Tips

### For 2 Characters (Child + 1 Supporting):
- Child: 150 words
- Supporting: 40-80 words (human 40w, animal 60-80w)
- Scene/Style: 100 words
- **Total**: ~300-330 words ✅

### For 3 Characters (Child + 2 Supporting):
- Child: 150 words
- Supporting 1: 40 words
- Supporting 2: 40-60 words
- Scene/Style: 80 words
- **Total**: ~310-350 words ✅

### For 4+ Characters (Large Family):
- Child: 150 words
- Each Supporting: 20-30 words (simplified)
- Scene/Style: 60 words
- **Total**: ~350-400 words (maximum) ⚠️

**Rule**: Never exceed 400 words total prompt length.

## Safety and Educational Guidelines

### Always Show:
- ✅ Gentle respectful interaction
- ✅ Safe distance for wild animals (observe, don't touch)
- ✅ Proper holding/petting for pets
- ✅ Realistic animal behavior
- ✅ Adult supervision (implied or visible) for young children

### Never Show:
- ❌ Rough handling
- ❌ Unsafe touching of wild animals
- ❌ Unrealistic anthropomorphized behavior in prompts
- ❌ Dangerous situations

### Educational Value:
- **Pets**: Responsibility, care, loyalty, companionship
- **Farm Animals**: Life cycles, agriculture, food sources, care
- **Wild Animals**: Ecosystems, habitats, observation skills, respect for nature
- **Insects**: Micro-nature, pollination, biodiversity, careful observation

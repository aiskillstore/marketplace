# Child-Centered Scenes Expansion

## New Scenes Based on Child Psychology and Chinese Culture

This document adds scenes that resonate with Chinese children's daily experiences and psychological development needs (3-6 years).

---

### 6. Home Kitchen (厨房) - `kitchen`

**Environment**: Warm家庭厨房 with cooking activities

**Visual Elements**:
- Warm wooden or tiled kitchen space
- Steaming woks and cooking utensils
- Fresh vegetables and ingredients
- Family cooking together atmosphere
- Traditional Chinese kitchen items (竹蒸笼, 菜刀, 砧板)

**Atmosphere**:
- Warm and familial
- Busy but organized
- Aromatic and inviting
- Eye-level child perspective

**Educational Focus**:
- Family bonds and cooperation
- Food preparation and nutrition
- Cultural cuisine appreciation
- Responsibility and helping

**Suggested Chinese Characters**:
- 饭 (fàn) - rice/meal
- 菜 (cài) - vegetable/dish
- 锅 (guō) - pot/wok
- 香 (xiāng) - fragrant
- 热 (rè) - hot

**Scene-Specific Details**:
```
warm home kitchen, steaming wok with aromatic food, fresh vegetables on wooden cutting board, traditional Chinese kitchen tools, cozy family cooking atmosphere, warm lighting from above
```

**Child Psychology**:
- **Security (安全感)**: Home environment, family presence
- **Participation (参与感)**: Helping adults, learning life skills
- **Sensory Learning**: Smell, sight, sound of cooking
- **Cultural Connection**: Traditional Chinese food culture

### 7. Courtyard/Siheyuan (四合院) - `courtyard`

**Environment**: Traditional Chinese courtyard with cultural elements

**Visual Elements**:
- Square courtyard with sky opening above
- Traditional Chinese architecture elements
- Potted plants and small garden
- Stone pathways and wooden structures
- Lanterns and traditional decorations
- Goldfish pond (optional)

**Atmosphere**:
- Peaceful and enclosed
- Culturally rich
- Safe and intimate
- Multi-generational warmth

**Educational Focus**:
- Traditional Chinese architecture
- Family living together (三代同堂)
- Outdoor-indoor connection
- Cultural heritage

**Suggested Chinese Characters**:
- 院 (yuàn) - courtyard
- 家 (jiā) - home/family
- 门 (mén) - door/gate
- 灯 (dēng) - lantern/light
- 花 (huā) - flower

**Scene-Specific Details**:
```
traditional Chinese courtyard, enclosed square space with sky above, red lanterns hanging, potted plants, stone pathways, wooden pillared structures, peaceful family atmosphere
```

**Child Psychology**:
- **Belonging (归属感)**: Extended family environment
- **Cultural Roots**: Connection to Chinese tradition
- **Safe Exploration**: Enclosed but spacious
- **Multi-sensory**: Architecture, nature, family life

---

### 8. Street Market (市场) - `market`

**Environment**: Bustling traditional Chinese street market

**Visual Elements**:
- Colorful vendor stalls with fresh produce
- Hanging lanterns and signs with characters
- People shopping and vendors calling
- Fresh fruits, vegetables, fish in baskets
- Traditional market architecture
- Red and gold decorative elements

**Atmosphere**:
- Lively and energetic
- Colorful and abundant
- Social and interactive
- Ground-level bustling activity

**Educational Focus**:
- Social interaction and community
- Numbers and counting (buying/selling)
- Food names and varieties
- Daily life observation

**Suggested Chinese Characters**:
- 买 (mǎi) - buy
- 卖 (mài) - sell
- 钱 (qián) - money
- 多 (duō) - many/much
- 少 (shǎo) - few/little

**Scene-Specific Details**:
```
bustling Chinese street market, colorful vendor stalls with fresh produce, hanging red lanterns, Chinese character signs, baskets of fruits and vegetables, lively crowd atmosphere, warm daylight
```

**Child Psychology**:
- **Social Learning**: Observing interactions
- **Sensory Richness**: Colors, sounds, smells
- **Real-world Math**: Counting, comparing
- **Cultural Daily Life**: Authentic Chinese experience

### 9. Temple/Pagoda (寺庙) - `temple`

**Environment**: Peaceful Chinese Buddhist temple or pagoda

**Visual Elements**:
- Traditional curved rooftops with upturned eaves
- Red pillars and golden accents
- Incense smoke rising gently
- Stone lion statues or guardians
- Peaceful courtyard with trees
- Prayer bells and decorative elements

**Atmosphere**:
- Serene and reverent
- Spiritually peaceful
- Architecturally magnificent
- Quiet and contemplative

**Educational Focus**:
- Cultural and spiritual heritage
- Respect and reverence (尊重)
- Traditional architecture appreciation
- Quietness and contemplation

**Suggested Chinese Characters**:
- 庙 (miào) - temple
- 塔 (tǎ) - pagoda/tower
- 香 (xiāng) - incense
- 钟 (zhōng) - bell
- 静 (jìng) - quiet/peaceful

**Scene-Specific Details**:
```
traditional Chinese temple, curved upturned roof eaves, red pillars with golden details, gentle incense smoke rising, stone lion guardians, peaceful courtyard with ancient trees, serene atmosphere
```

**Child Psychology**:
- **Quiet Time**: Learning to be calm and peaceful
- **Awe and Wonder**: Impressive architecture
- **Respect**: Cultural and spiritual respect
- **Cultural Heritage**: Connection to traditions

### 10. Festival Celebration (节日庆典) - `festival`

**Environment**: Joyful Chinese festival celebration scene

**Visual Elements**:
- Red lanterns hanging everywhere
- Decorative couplets (春联) on doors
- Firecrackers and festive decorations
- Special festival foods displayed
- People in festive clothing
- Vibrant red and gold colors

**Atmosphere**:
- Joyful and celebratory
- Crowded but happy
- Colorful and decorated
- Festive energy and excitement

**Educational Focus**:
- Chinese festivals and traditions
- Celebration and joy
- Family gathering importance
- Cultural customs and meanings

**Suggested Chinese Characters**:
- 节 (jié) - festival/holiday
- 庆 (qìng) - celebrate
- 红 (hóng) - red
- 福 (fú) - fortune/blessing
- 灯 (dēng) - lantern

**Scene-Specific Details**:
```
Chinese festival celebration, countless red lanterns hanging, spring couplets on doors, festive decorations, joyful crowd, traditional festival foods, vibrant red and gold colors, celebratory atmosphere
```

**Child Psychology**:
- **Joy and Excitement**: High positive emotion
- **Family Bonding**: Gathering and celebration
- **Cultural Pride**: Participating in traditions
- **Anticipation**: Festival excitement and rituals

warm traditional Chinese elder's room, comfortable wooden furniture, embroidered quilts and pillows, family photos on wall, traditional tea set, soft warm lighting, cozy nostalgic atmosphere

## Child Development Alignment

### Emotional Development (情感发展)

**Security and Attachment** (安全感与依恋):
- Kitchen, Grandma Room, Courtyard, Kindergarten

**Social Skills** (社交能力):
- Market, Festival, Kindergarten

**Cultural Identity** (文化认同):
- Temple, Courtyard, Festival, Market

### Cognitive Development (认知发展)

**Observation Skills** (观察力):
- Market (variety), Temple (details), Kindergarten (organization)

**Categorization** (分类能力):
- Kitchen (food types), Market (vendor categories)

**Spatial Awareness** (空间意识):
- Courtyard (enclosed space), Temple (architecture)

### Moral Development (道德发展)

**Respect** (尊重):
- Temple (reverence), Grandma Room (filial piety)

**Sharing and Cooperation** (分享与合作):
- Kitchen (helping), Kindergarten (playing together)

## Usage Recommendations

### Age 3-4 (最适合3-4岁):
- Kitchen, Grandma Room, Kindergarten (familiar, safe)
- Meadow, Pond (simple, clear nature)

### Age 4-5 (最适合4-5岁):
- Market, Festival (more stimulation, social)
- Forest, Rice Paddy (more complex nature)
- Courtyard (cultural understanding emerging)

### Age 5-6 (最适合5-6岁):
- Temple (contemplative capacity developing)
- All scenes (full range accessible)
- Multi-page stories across multiple scenes

## Cultural Education Goals

By including these scenes, children learn:
1. **Daily Life** (日常生活): Kitchen, Market
2. **Architecture** (建筑): Courtyard, Temple
3. **Traditions** (传统): Festival, Temple, Grandma Room
4. **Social Environments** (社交环境): Market, Kindergarten
5. **Family Bonds** (家庭纽带): Kitchen, Courtyard, Grandma Room

This comprehensive scene library supports both **nature exploration** and **cultural immersion** for holistic child development.

# Email Campaign Skill Operating Checklist

Use this checklist when the request involves campaign briefs, audience logic, creative QA, launch readiness, and post-send analysis or could affect a live email system.

## Intake

- Campaign objective, offer, audience, send date, and owner
- Segment rules, exclusions, suppression logic, and estimated count
- Subject, preview, body copy, landing page, assets, and tracking plan
- Historical campaign benchmarks and current deliverability context
- Approval requirements for brand, legal, product, and operations

## QA

- Confirm the brief, audience, creative, and landing page all match.
- Confirm exclusions protect recent buyers, unsubscribes, suppressions, and sensitive cohorts.
- Confirm links, UTM parameters, merge tags, and fallbacks work.
- Confirm test send rendering is acceptable for target clients.
- Confirm the retrospective captures what to keep, change, test, or stop.

## Risk Gates

Low risk: research, summaries, drafts, critiques, naming, and non-production recommendations.

Medium risk: template edits, segment recommendations, automation diagrams, experiments, imports prepared for review, and code changes that need deployment.

High risk: live sends, contact imports, suppression edits, DNS/authentication changes, production automation changes, provider migrations, and destructive cleanup. Stop and request explicit approval before high-risk actions.

## Primary Sources

### ICT (Inner Circle Trader) - Michael J. Huddleston

The original source of most SMC concepts.

**Free YouTube Content:**
- ICT YouTube Channel: https://www.youtube.com/@InnerCircleTrader
- 2022 Mentorship (free): Comprehensive series covering all concepts
- ICT Charter Price Action Model: Core methodology explained

**Key Playlists:**
- "2022 ICT Mentorship" - Most structured, comprehensive
- "ICT Forex" - Original teachings
- "ICT Futures" - Applied to futures markets

**Note:** ICT's teaching style is verbose. Expect 1-2 hour videos. The 2022 mentorship is considered the most organized presentation of his concepts.

## Books

### Directly Related to SMC/ICT

**"The Inner Circle Trader's Price Action Model"** - Study guides compiled from ICT teachings
- Not an official book, but community-compiled study materials
- Available in various trading communities

### Market Microstructure (Academic Foundation)

**"Trading and Exchanges: Market Microstructure for Practitioners"** by Larry Harris
- Academic but accessible
- Explains how markets actually work
- Essential for understanding WHY liquidity hunting exists
- Oxford University Press, 2003
- ISBN: 978-0195144703

**"Market Microstructure Theory"** by Maureen O'Hara
- More academic/technical
- Deep dive into order flow and price formation
- Blackwell Publishers, 1995
- ISBN: 978-0631207610

### Wyckoff Method (Conceptual Predecessor)

SMC builds heavily on Wyckoff. Understanding Wyckoff helps understand SMC's logic.

**"Trades About to Happen"** by David Weis
- Modern interpretation of Wyckoff
- Focus on price/volume relationships
- Wiley, 2013
- ISBN: 978-0470487808

**"The Wyckoff Methodology in Depth"** by Rub√©n Villahermosa
- Comprehensive Wyckoff guide
- Clear explanations of accumulation/distribution
- Available on Amazon, multiple editions

**"Studies in Tape Reading"** by Richard Wyckoff
- Original source material
- Historical but foundational
- Public domain, available free online

### Order Flow & Volume Profile

**"A Complete Guide to Volume Price Analysis"** by Anna Coulling
- Accessible introduction
- Connects volume to price action
- ISBN: 978-1491249390

**"Markets in Profile"** by James Dalton
- Market Profile methodology
- Understanding value areas and balance
- Wiley, 2007
- ISBN: 978-0470039489

### Auction Market Theory

**"Mind Over Markets"** by James Dalton
- Auction market theory applied to trading
- Understanding market-generated information
- Wiley, 1999
- ISBN: 978-1118531730

### Psychology & Execution

**"Trading in the Zone"** by Mark Douglas
- Trading psychology classic
- Understanding probabilistic thinking
- Prentice Hall, 2000
- ISBN: 978-0735201446

## Recommended Learning Path

### Phase 1: Foundation (2-4 weeks)
1. Complete BabyPips school (free)
2. Read Larry Harris book (or summaries)
3. Understand basic candlestick patterns

### Phase 2: SMC Core Concepts (4-8 weeks)
1. ICT 2022 Mentorship (YouTube) - watch all
2. Take detailed notes
3. Practice identifying structures on charts

### Phase 3: Application (Ongoing)
1. Daily chart markup practice
2. Build this skill's detection scripts
3. Paper trade using SMC analysis
4. Journal and review

### Phase 4: Refinement
1. Study Wyckoff for deeper understanding
2. Read auction market theory books
3. Develop personal trading plan

## Critical Perspective

**Important:** SMC/ICT is a framework, not a guaranteed system. Consider:

1. **Survivorship bias** - You see successful traders, not failures
2. **Self-fulfilling prophecy** - Patterns work partly because many watch them
3. **Cherry-picked examples** - Education often shows perfect setups
4. **Risk management** - No analysis replaces proper risk control

The detection algorithms in this skill identify patterns. Whether those patterns have predictive value in your specific market/timeframe requires your own validation and backtesting.
